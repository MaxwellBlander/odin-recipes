ORGANIC BAKED RUSSET POTATOES

Description

Russet potatoes are the best for baking due to their thick skins that cook cripsy. They are a classic staple that has many benefits from providing energy to aiding in muscle recovery. As potatoes are grown with high pesticides I would reccomend buying organic potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes
  • Coconut/Olive Oil
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Rub potatoes clean of dirt under running water.
  2. Cut out the root eye like nodules from the potatoe to reduce solanine intake as well as the stem connection at the tip of the potatoes.
  3. Stab a hole through each potatoe with a knife or poke some holes with a fork to ensure they dont burst in the oven
  4. Oil up the potatoes in your choice of coconut oil or olive oil
  5. Preheat the oven to 450
  6. Place the potatoes in the oven on a metal tray lined with tin foil
  7. Allow the potatoes to sit in the oven for 45-55 minutes at 450 degrees F and do not cover them. There is also no need to flip them
  8. Remove the potatoes from the oven and spice them with onion powder. Use caution for they will be hot!
