Homestyle Pork Ribs

Description

Pork ribs are delicious and a dish you do not want to miss out on in your lifetime. They take time to cook and are worth the reward!

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs
  • Dry rub/seasoning

Cooking Steps

  1. Cover metal tray with aliminum foil and place a cooking rack ontop so the ribs are elevated and do not cook in their own juices.
  2. Apply dry rub or seasoning to the ribs.
  3. Place the ribs bone-side down on the rack.
  4. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees fahrenheit on bake and let the ribs cook for 2 hours.
  5. Raise the temperature to 350 degrees fahrenheit on bake and allow the ribs to cook for another hour.
  6. Serve and enjoy! Use caution for they will be hot!
