Turkey Burgers

Description

Turkey burgers to perfection a great source of protein and easy to cook!

Ingredients

  • Turkey meat ground or pre rounded
  • Tumeric
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Remove turkey burgers from packaging
  2. Form the raw meat into round patties if not already
  3. Place the patties onto a metal tray lined with tin foil over a metal grill
  4. Set oven to broil, high and put the meat in
  5. Set timer to 17 minutes and wait
  6. Flip the turkey burgers when the timer rings and set the timer again, this time to 11 minutes
  7. Remove the meat from the oven and spice with garlic powder and tumeric
