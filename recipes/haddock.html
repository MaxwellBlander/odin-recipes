Baked Haddock

Description

Haddock is a delicious white fish that is easily cooked and makes for a great source of protein.

  • Haddock
  • Olive oil
  • Spices of your choosing

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash the fish under running water.
  2. Cover a metal tray with aluminum foil.
  3. Coat the foil with a thin layer of olive oil not directly to the fish as doing so will make the edges cook quicker than the rest of the fish.
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit on the bake setting.
  5. Place the fish in the oven and allow to cook for 15-17 minutes.
  6. Remove the fish from the oven and apply your choice of spices. Use caution for it will be hot!
