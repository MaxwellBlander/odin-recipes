Sausage

Description

Sausage is a delicious and easy to cook dish.

Ingredients

  • Sausage of your choosing
  • Spices of your choosing

Cooking Steps

  1. Remove sausage from packaging and place onto a metal tray covered in tin foil with a grill on top
  2. Set oven to broil on high.
  3. Set timer to 9 minutes.
  4. Place sausages in the oven and wait for the timer to ring
  5. Flip sausages when the timer rings and set for another 9 minutes
  6. Remove them from the oven and apply spice. Use caution for they will be hot!
